Hotel and Transportation

Hotel

All conference events will be located in the South Convention Center, Level 3 at Mandalay Bay. Mandalay Bay is located at 3950 Las Vegas Boulevard South, in the heart of the Las Vegas Strip. Mandalay Bay features spacious accommodations, a full-service spa, a world-famous aquatic playground and a host of gaming and entertainment options. For a limited time, Kaseya Connect 2014 attendees are eligible for a special rate of $89 per night from April 13 through April 16. Pre-conference training attendees are eligible for a special weekend rate of $139 per night for April 11-12 for a limited time. Click the link below to reserve online.

Transportation

Mandalay Bay is located 2 miles from McCarran International Airport - approximately a 10-15 minute drive, depending on traffic. For assistance in booking ground transportation, please contact the Mandalay Bay concierge desk at +1 (702) 632-4760.

Parking

Mandalay Bay offers complimentary parking.

Weather

During April, daily high temperatures average a warm and sunny 78 degrees Fahrenheit (~26 degrees Celsius) with evening temperatures in the low to mid 50s Fahrenheit (~10 degrees Celsius), so pack accordingly!
