Fundamentals Training Course

Saturday, April 27th - Sunday April 28th • 9am-6pm

Kaseya Fundamentals Education Workshop (regularly $1,500) is technical training targeted to engineers and operational managers. The course is designed to optimize interaction between participants in a technical discussion surrounding a successful Kaseya implementation.

Fundamentals includes the core functionality and usage of Kaseya including Agent Deployment, Agent Procedures, Audit & Inventory, Info Center, Live Connect, Monitoring, Patch Management, Remote Control, System Setup and Ticketing.

 $995

Service Desk Training

Sunday, April 28th • 1-6pm

Service Desk Training (regularly $695) is targeted for system administrators responsible for setting up/configuring and customizing the Service Desk for their IT organization. Service Desk Administration covers configuration elements including Workflow definitions, data fields, roles, permissions and automation.

Students who complete either course will have the opportunity to complete the certification exam to become Kaseya Certified.

 $395

AntFarm Industry Expert Workshop

Designing Services That Perform - Creating the Service Blueprint for Profitable Growth

Sunday, April 28th • 10am - 5pm

Designing Services That Perform (regularly $995) is a powerful workshop focusing on developing services that will be truly valuable to both your customers and your business. This workshop will provide you a series of techniques and tools that will allow you to create a blueprinted service based on the needs of your customers. Upon completion you will know what your customers value from your services, have the tools to design your services around consistency, and have the ability to institutionalize your competitive advantage.
