FAQ

Where will Kaseya Connect be held?
Kaseya Connect will be held at Mandalay Bay in Las Vegas, Nevada, USA.

Where is the hotel located?
Mandalay Bay is located at 3950 Las Vegas Boulevard South in Las Vegas, Nevada, a drive of approximately 10-15 minutes from McCarran International Airport.

How do I make sleeping room reservations?
It should be: Visit the conference reservations page to book your sleeping room online. The special conference rate of $89 per night is available for a limited time.

What do I do for transportation?
Mandalay Bay is located 2 miles from McCarran International Airport - approximately a 10-15 minute drive, depending on traffic. For assistance in booking ground transportation, please contact the Mandalay Bay concierge desk at +1 (702) 632-4760.

How do I obtain my badge?
Attendees may pick up their badges onsite at the Kaseya Connect registration desk at Mandalay Bay.

What is the dress code?
Business casual attire is recommended for all conference events.

How much does it cost to attend Kaseya Connect?
The standard rate for attending Kaseya Connect is $895 USD. Attendees who register by February 15 are eligible for the early bird discount rate of $695 USD, a $200 savings.

How much does it cost to attend the pre-conference training?
Pre-conference training will also be offered in conjunction with Kaseya Connect on April 12-13. Experienced Kaseya administrators can prepare to become a Kaseya Certified Administrator (KCA) with our Review, Test Prep and Exam course for $995. Those new to Kaseya can get on the path to certification with our Quick Start Bootcamp for $1500, a $2500 value. Only a limited number of training seats are available.

What is included in the registration fee?
The conference fee includes attendance at the event including access to all main stage and track sessions, conference meals, and attendance at conference special events. Attendees are responsible for the cost of their flights, accommodations, transfers and incidentals.

How do I become a sponsor?
Kaseya Connect is the ideal venue to showcase partner offerings to the Kaseya user community. Numerous sponsorship opportunities are available at Kaseya Connect in a variety of investment levels. To inquire about sponsorship, please send an email to vivalasvegas@kaseya.com.

How do I become a presenter?
Please email your proposed session topic and speaker information to vivalasvegas@kaseya.com to be considered for a speaking opportunity at Kaseya Connect. All available speaking opportunities are 45-60 minutes in length and will take place in Las Vegas, Nevada on April 14, 15 or 16, 2014. Kaseya reserves the right to reject any and all proposed session topics. Kaseya will only follow up on session proposals that are under consideration for inclusion at Kaseya Connect.

What is your cancellation/substitution policy?
Attendees may cancel their registration without penalty prior to March 14, 2014. Substitutions may be made through April 9, 2014. To edit or cancel your registration, visit the Registration Portal and log in using the confirmation number you received during your initial registration.

Who can I contact with additional questions about Kaseya Connect?
Email your question to vivalasvegas@kaseya.com.
