    Keynote: Business Re-Imagined

    South Seas Ballroom

    Mike Walsh, Founder and CEO, Tomorrow

    What does it take to lead a 21st century IT organization? New technology is not only an opportunity to improve productivity, it invites us to ask a much bigger question - can we re-imagine the way we do business? During this session, Mike will discuss some of the most disruptive trends facing IT organizations today, and the new models of innovation that are required to survive in this fast paced and uncertain environment. He will cover the impact of Cloud computing, big data, enterprise mobility, consumerization, and more, leaving you with a roadmap for surviving disruptive change.

    Mike Walsh is the Founder and CEO of Tomorrow, a consumer innovation research lab. He advises some of the world's leading brands and corporations on what's coming next in their industry. Mike is a leading authority on the intersection of emerging technologies and fast growth markets, and his views have appeared in a wide range of international publications including Business Week, Forbes and Wallpaper Magazine.
