Hotel Information

Four Seasons Las Vegas

Small, quiet and residential, the Four Seasons Hotel Las Vegas is a non-gaming oasis of tranquility, immersed within the city’s thrill and spectacle.

Located at the Strip’s south end, the Four Seasons is situated within the Mandalay Bay Hotel and Casino complex, yet is completely independent, featuring its own private driveway, lobby and amenities. Perched high above the strip on the 35th to 39th floors of the Mandalay Bay tower, guests are ensured consistently excellent views.

Maps and City Information

For information about activities and attractions in Las Vegas, visit the Four Seasons Hotel Concierge. For maps and additional information, visit the Hotel’s web site at: www.fourseasons.com/lasvegas

Airport Transportation

The Four Seasons is approximately 15 minutes from the McCarran International Airport. A taxi between the airport and hotel costs approximately $20.00 USD plus tip. Rental cars are available at the McCarran International Airport; please contact your preferred rental car agency directly. Valet parking at the Four Seasons is available for a fee of $19.00 per night.

Business Center

The Four Seasons has a full-service business center located on the 2nd floor of the hotel.

Lost and Found

Lost and found articles can be delivered to or retrieved from the Kaseya Connect registration area in the Mesquite Foyer.

ATM Location

The ATM is located in the hotel on the lobby level by the event salon restrooms.
