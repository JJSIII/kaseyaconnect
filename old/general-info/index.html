General Information

Conference Registration

The registration desk is located on the 2nd floor in the Mesquite Foyer.

Name Badges

Your name badge is required for admission to all conference sessions, Pavilion, and events. Your badge must be visible at all times.

Meals and Meeting Functions

All meeting functions will be held at the Four Seasons. General Session will be held in the Four Seasons Ballroom. Please join us pool side Monday evening at the Four Seasons for the Kaseya Connect Welcome Reception & Pool Party. On Tuesday evening the Mandalay Bay’s House of Blues will transform for Kaseya Jam.

Partner Pavilion

The Partner Pavilion is located in the Four Seasons Ballroom Foyer. This year's exhibition includes more Partners than ever before. Come to the Partner Pavilion to learn about the latest products and services.

Attire

The suggested attire for this year’s conference in Las Vegas is business casual for all conference functions. Pants, slacks, collared shirts, and skirts are all acceptable.

Cell Phones

Please be considerate of other attendees and session presenters by turning your cell phones off, or to silent/vibrate mode, during all sessions.
